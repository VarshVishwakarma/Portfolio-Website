WHY FASTAPI?

Selected for its native asynchronous capabilities (ASGI), allowing non-blocking handling of AI requests. Pydantic models ensure strict data validation for the Chat Interface, preventing injection attacks and malformed payloads.

WHY GROQ?

Groq's LPU (Language Processing Unit) architecture delivers tokens at ~300 T/s, significantly faster than standard GPUs. This eliminates the "lag" typical of AI chat, preserving the immersive "Real-Time Terminal" feel of this portfolio.
